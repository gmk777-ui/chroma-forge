How LCForge AI works
A three‑step workflow from drug query to chromatographic method, literature context, and physicochemical understanding.
1

Enter drug & context

Provide drug name, sample type (API, formulation, plasma), and analytical technique (HPLC or LC‑MS / MS).

2

AI generates a draft method

In production, LCForge AI would propose column, mobile phase, pH, gradient, and LC‑MS parameters aligned with QbD and White Analytical Chemistry.

3

Review, optimize, export

Scientists refine the method, review literature, inspect drug properties, and export a structured method report for validation.
